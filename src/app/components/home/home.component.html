How to configure a question

1. Click on Quiz configuration

2. Press Modify button in one question

3. Write some text in the Part 1 text field (example: My daughter)

4. Write the correct answer in the Part 2 text field (example: runs)

5. Write the final part of the sentence in the Part 3 text field (example: faster than me (run))

6. Press button Apply changes to save

7. Click on take quiz to see your change
